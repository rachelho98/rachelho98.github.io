About Me

Rachel holds a M.A. in Developmental Psychology and is a Research Assistant at NYU Play and Language Lab who is passionate in bridging early childhood development research to practice. After working as an early childhood educator, she wanted to combine her love for curriculum development, early education research, and design thinking to create opportunities for high-quality parent-child engagement and learning through media.
